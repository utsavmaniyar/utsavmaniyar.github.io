About JY-Food

We started our journey four years back participating in Stavik food festival and continued the idea of healthy homemade food and dilwali special sweets. Hoping to make people enjoy rare unheard yet healthy food.

History of our journey

We have started JY-Foods with a great mission to support cancer patients. Initially we have started sweets on Diwali festival(Year 2015). We have donated 50% of our profit to cancer patient. Since then, every year we are selling sweets on Diwali.

We have also started writing special food recipes for mouth cancer patients, which are easy to eat, digest and healthy. Recipe Book is completed and it's under review proceeds.
